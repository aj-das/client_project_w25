Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) supports the health and well-being of the University of Michigan community. We aim to help students cope with life stresses, realize their potential, and build meaningful connections.

CAPS offers both in-person and virtual services, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all. Our Front Desk, located at the Michigan Union 4th Floor, is open to assist you. Contact us at (734) 764-8312 for more information.

Our Services

  • Clinical Services: Individual counseling, crisis support, and more
  • Outreach Programs: Workshops, peer support groups, and events
  • Teletherapy: Virtual sessions through Uwill
  • Togetherall: Online, anonymous peer-support platform
